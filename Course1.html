Course Description

In today's digital age, personal data is a valuable asset. This course equips you with the knowledge and skills to become a champion for data privacy within your organization. Designed for professionals seeking to fulfil the role of a Data Protection Officer (DPO) as mandated by upcoming data privacy regulations in India, the program provides a thorough understanding of key concepts like data privacy principles, rights of data subjects, and obligations of data controllers.

Participants will gain practical strategies for implementing robust data protection management programs, including conducting data protection impact assessments, developing watertight data protection policies, and managing data breaches effectively. Through case studies, scholarly materials, and lectures by experts, you will understand the holistic role of a Data Protection Officer within an organization.



Career Prospects

+
-

The demand for skilled Data Protection Officers is growing rapidly as organizations strive to comply with evolving data protection regulations. Completing this course will prepare you for a rewarding career as a DPO, equipping you with the expertise to safeguard organizations against legal risks and reputational damage associated with non-compliance and data breaches. Whether you're a legal professional, compliance officer, IT security professional, or business manager, this course will enhance your ability to navigate complex data protection laws and practices.

Course Aims and Objectives

+
-

- Provide a comprehensive understanding of the legal framework surrounding data protection in India, and other relevant international legal frameworks.

- Master key data privacy concepts such as data subject rights, data minimization, and lawful processing of personal data.

- Develop practical skills for implementing a data protection management program within an organization.

- Learn to conduct data protection impact assessments (DPIAs) to identify and mitigate potential data privacy risks.

- Craft effective data protection policies and procedures that comply with legal requirements and best practices.

- Develop a comprehensive understanding of data breach management processes and response strategies.

- Sharpen communication and training skills to effectively raise awareness and educate stakeholders within an organization about data privacy.

- Learn best practices for handling data subject requests and ensuring compliance with their rights.
